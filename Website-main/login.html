About Pothole Rescue

Pothole Rescue is Barbados's dedicated road condition management platform,helping drivers navigate safely and empowering road authorities to make data-driven repair decisions.

Our Mission

Connect everyday drivers with real-time road data and give the Ministry of Transport the tools needed to keep Barbados's roads safe.

Who Uses It?

🚗Customers

Plan safer routes and report hazards.

🏛️Government

Oversee repair costs and road reports.

🔧IT / Maintenance

Manage data and monitor system health.
