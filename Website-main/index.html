📖 How to Use Pothole Rescue

🚗

Welcome to Pothole Rescue

Barbados's road management platform. Track potholes, plan safer routes, and help authorities prioritise repairs all in one place.

🗺️

Step 1: Plan Your Route

Click Plan Route in the sidebar. Type a road name or click Browse Roads to pick from a popup list. An interactive Barbados map will highlight the road.

📊

Step 2: Read the Analysis

Results show pothole density, depth breakdown (shallow / medium / deep), estimated costs, and a decay forecast. Cards are colour-coded by severity.

📉

Step 3: Monitor Decay

The Decay tab shows an animated progress bar filling toward the 50% critical threshold, plus a week-by-week projection chart.

⚙️

Step 4: Customise

Use Settings to switch Light / Dark / High Contrast themes, adjust text size, enable dyslexia-friendly font, and set alert thresholds.

✅

You're Ready!

Close this guide and start exploring Barbados's road network. The Contact page is always available if you need help.

ℹ️ About Us

Our Mission

Pothole Rescue is dedicated to enhancing road safety across Barbados by providing real-time pothole data, route planning tools, and actionable insights for drivers and road authorities.

Standards & Quality

We adhere to the highest standards of data accuracy, user privacy, and accessibility. Our platform is built with modern web technologies to ensure reliability and performance.

Who We Serve

From everyday drivers planning safer routes to government officials monitoring infrastructure, Pothole Rescue empowers all users with the information they need to make informed decisions.
